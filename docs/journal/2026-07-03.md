### CI fix — smoke test startup timing (PR #77)

Staging smoke test was failing `GET /health expected 200, got 503` immediately
after deploy. Root cause: `flyctl deploy --wait-timeout` waits for the container
to be **running** (TCP port open), not for NestJS to finish **initializing**.
As the app module grew (Phase 3 modules), initialization took longer.

**Fix 1** — `deploy-staging.yml`: added `sleep 15` before the smoke test step.
Still failed (503) — 15 s wasn't enough on a cold Fly.io machine.

**Fix 2** (same PR, second commit) — two changes:
- `deploy-staging.yml`: `sleep 15` → `sleep 30`
- `scripts/smoke-test.sh` op 1: replaced single-shot curl with a retry loop
  (up to 10 attempts × 5 s = 50 s retry window)

Total cold-start budget: 30 s fixed + up to 50 s retry = **80 s maximum**.
The retry loop also makes the smoke test self-healing against transient
blips that would otherwise fail the whole deploy.
